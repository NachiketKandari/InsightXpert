# Leadership Questions

**Q: List three specific leadership or business questions that your system should be able to answer using the provided dataset schema.**

---

**1. (Risk/Operational):** Which sender banks and device types show a disproportionately high fraud-flag rate during late-night hours (22:00–04:00) on weekends, and how does this compare to their overall baseline?
→ Helps leadership prioritise real-time monitoring rules and allocate investigation resources to the highest-risk bank×device×time segments.

**2. (Behavioral/Segmentation):** For P2M transactions in the 18–25 age group, what is the month-over-month shift in merchant-category spend share (Food vs Entertainment vs Shopping), and do these shifts differ across 4G vs 5G users?
→ Lets leadership tailor merchant partnerships and cashback campaigns to the highest lifetime-value growth segment.

**3. (Strategic/Trend):** Which five states show the fastest quarter-over-quarter growth in P2P transaction volume, and what is the corresponding trend in average transaction amount and failure rate in those states?
→ Rapid volume growth with rising failure rates signals infrastructure strain; leadership needs this combined view to decide where to invest in capacity and partnerships.
